# 📦 BizLedger Pro

**BizLedger Pro** is a powerful, offline-first business management suite designed for small shopkeepers and entrepreneurs. It helps you manage your business with zero internet required.

## 🚀 Key Features
* 🚨 **Business Alerts:** Automatic notifications for low stock and high customer debt.
* 📞 **Contact Manager:** Keep all your customer mobile numbers in one place.
* 📦 **Inventory Tracker:** Real-time tracking of your shop items.
* 💰 **Daily Ledger:** Simple credit/debit entry system to track your cash flow.

## 🛠️ How to Run
1. Go to the **Releases** section on the right.
2. Download the `BizLedgerPro.exe` file.
3. Move the file to a dedicated folder on your Desktop.
4. Double-click to start managing your business!

*Note: Your data is saved locally in .txt files within the same folder. Keep them safe!*
